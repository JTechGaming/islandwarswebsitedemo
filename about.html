About Islandwars

The full story about Islandwars.

Islandwars is a Minecraft Server project designed to provide a modded feel for a vanilla client.
The project was founded in 2021 and started growing in 2022. The team currently exists of
4 people but were looking to expand the team for a better result. The project will
go open-source upon completion of the official SMP Server, where we will invite some YouTubers to
come and play on there for a few months! All players will release videos/episodes of the series!
The project is owned and managed by the one and only JTechGaming. If you would like to
support the project and want to help us out, than you can consider purchasing
an item from the merch store, or donating! Any donations/purchases are much apreciated! If you want
to know more about the playing experience, make sure to continue reading this page, follow the newsletter,
read the blogposts, and subscribe to JTechGaming's YouTube Channel!
